About FoodCubo

We are India's one of the most promising food delivery portal, started by Rohit Kashyap. Originating from the city of Patna we have a rage to spread its services across India. FoodCubo focuses on easy access of food through FoodCubo App or Website. Customer can order through our Platform from a wide range of Selected Restaurant. We are also planning to come with our tiffin service. Our Tiffin Service is something unique it is not like Just an simple and old monthly subscription based service. Our tiffin is just like restaurant style in which local housewife and food vendor can register and sell their food through our app. We provide A single window for ordering from a wide range of restaurants & Food Vendors, we have our own exclusive fleet of delivery personnel to pickup orders from restaurants and deliver it to customers

Why FoodCubo

Having our own fleet of delivery boys gives us the flexibility to offer customers a no minimum order policy on any restaurant and accept online payments for all partner restaurants and food vendors that we work with. Our delivery personnel carry one order at a time which ensures we get reliable and fast deliveries for cities like Patna, Muzaffarpur, Bhagalpur , Darbhanga and other cities and towns of Bihar "Deliver and serve the freshest, most delicious meals quickly and courteously in modern, well-organized manner."
